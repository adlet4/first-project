Bloodborne

Bloodborne follows a Hunter through the decrepit Gothic, Victorian-era–inspired city of Yharnam,
whose inhabitants are afflicted with a blood-borne disease which transforms the residents, called
Yharnamites, into horrific beasts.

Bloodborne is an action role-playing game played from a third-person perspective and features
elements similar to those found in the Dark Souls series, also made by FromSoftware.
